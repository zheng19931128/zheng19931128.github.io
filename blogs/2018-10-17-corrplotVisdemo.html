CorrplotVis Intro

CorrplotVis is a function in corrplot package bulit on package htmlwigets. It is convenient to use corrplotVis to visualize the correlation matrix. You do not need understand a lot of parameters of corrplot function and just click the buttons to know more information about the correlation matrix. CorrplotVis is also good at the interacting with users. You can easliy insert interactive correlation matrix chart in html or rmarkdown, since corrplotVis bulit on d3.js.

The authors of corrplotVis function are Yang Zhou & Muzhi Yang

Finally, there is a lot of bugs in corrplotVis and I(Muzhi Yang) have not finish it yet. If you find some bugs or some goodideas, let me know !!!
